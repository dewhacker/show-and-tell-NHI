Roswell ↔ White Sands / Trinity proximity

Roswell, NM to Trinity Site (first nuclear detonation, July 16, 1945): ~120 miles southwest
Roswell to White Sands Missile Range (northern boundary): ~100 miles west
Roswell to Alamogordo / Holloman AFB: ~115 miles southwest
All within the same south-central New Mexico corridor

Why this matters for the talk

The alleged Roswell crash: early July 1947 — less than 2 years after Trinity
At that moment, Roswell Army Air Field was home to the 509th Bomb Group — the only nuclear-armed military unit in the world. The same unit that dropped the bombs on Hiroshima and Nagasaki
Roswell AAF housed the U.S. atomic bomb stockpile in 1947
White Sands was actively testing captured V-2 rockets (Operation Paperclip) and developing missile tech throughout 1946-47
So the "crash" allegedly happened in the literal backyard of: the world's only nuclear strike force, the active nuclear test region, and America's most advanced rocket/missile program
